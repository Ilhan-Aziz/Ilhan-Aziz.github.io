Additively Manufactured CubeSat Mirrors with a Focus on Manufacturing, Post Processing and Testing

UK Research and Innovation Industrial Placement Project
July 2024 to July 2025

Redesigning the primary mirror (M1) of the CCam earth observation cubesat for additive manufacture. A low cost imaging system for cubesat's developed by the UKATC and RAL Space.

CCam Optomechanics section view
